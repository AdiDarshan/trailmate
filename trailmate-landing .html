FAQ

Questions, answered.

Does TrailMate work for places outside Israel?

Right now, TrailMate is laser-focused on Israel — every trail, zimmer, and weather signal is tuned for the country. International expansion is on the roadmap.

Can I plan in Hebrew?

Yes. Chat in Hebrew or English — TrailMate replies in the same language and produces itineraries in either, including a Hebrew RTL PDF.

How accurate is the weather?

TrailMate uses live forecast APIs at trailhead-level resolution. We refresh the forecast 24 hours before your trip and notify you if the plan should change.

Does it book the zimmer for me?

In beta, we surface availability and link out to booking. Direct in-app booking with our partner network is coming next.

How much will it cost?

Free during beta. After launch, expect a generous free tier (a few plans per month) and a small monthly subscription for unlimited trips and premium features.
